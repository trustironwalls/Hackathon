GENERAL HINTS

Flags always look like: FLAG{some_text_here}

This lab has a full simulated Kali Linux filesystem. Explore every directory, especially hidden ones.

Useful starting points:

  • Always ls -la — never just ls
  • Check /etc, /var, /opt, /tmp
  • Read dotfiles: .bashrc .bash_history .profile
  • Some flags are encoded — try base64 -d, rev, tr
  • Use find / -name "*.txt" 2>/dev/null to enumerate
  • grep is your best friend for big files
  • Some flags require combining output from multiple files

Click a flag in the Flags tab for a specific hint.
